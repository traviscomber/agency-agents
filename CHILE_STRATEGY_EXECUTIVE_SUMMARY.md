## The "Why Now" for Chile

### **Market Conditions Are Perfect**

**1. Digital Adoption is High**
- 68% of Chilean SMBs already use cloud tools (SaaS, accounting software, CRM)
- Infrastructure is there; they're not tech-phobic
- They just need vertical solutions, not education on cloud

**2. Pain is Acute**
- Chilean economy is slower-growing; margins are tighter
- SMBs are resource-constrained (can't hire finance + compliance + sales ops teams)
- Regulatory complexity is increasing (labor law, environmental, procurement)
- Manual processes are eating 20-40% of operational time per function

**3. Payback is Fast**
- Recovery value is immediate (cash, revenue, compliance savings)
- Not a "nice-to-have" but a "must-have" once they see the ROI
- 30-90 day payback = fast decision cycle

**4. Channel Partners Exist**
- 8,000+ accountants (Contador público) in Chile ready to resell
- 2,000+ HR consultants
- Industry associations (ASEXMA, SOFOFA, CÁMARA) with built-in channels
- No territory conflicts; partner ecosystem is underdeveloped
