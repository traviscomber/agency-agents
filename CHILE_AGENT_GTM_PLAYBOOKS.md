## Playbook #1: Twin de Ejecutivo Comercial B2B

### **Target Segments (Priority Order)**

#### 1. **IT Services & Consultorías** (Highest ROI)
- **Segment size:** ~400 firms in Chile (10-100 people)
- **Pain point:** "We need 3-5 deals/month to hit quota; currently we're at 1-2"
- **Current process:** Email follow-ups, spreadsheet pipeline, no discipline
- **Agent impact:** Pipeline clarity + cadence = 50% more closed deals

**Messaging:**
> "Your sales team is losing deals in the follow-up phase. We give them a virtual pipeline manager who knows the next move for every account—every day."

**Entry conversation:**
- "How many active opportunities are you tracking right now?"
- "How long is your typical sales cycle?"
- "How many deals slip because of poor follow-up?"

**Pilot offer:**
- Free 30-day trial (manage 1 sales team, up to 20 active deals)
- No integration needed (copy-paste leads + emails weekly)
- Success metric: "We want to see +20% pipeline velocity by day 30"

**Expansion path:**
- $99/month/sales rep → 5-rep team (Typical IT Services firm)
- Add: Deal scoring, proposal templates, objection library

## Playbook #2: Twin de Analista de Licitaciones

### **Target Segments (Priority Order)**

#### 1. **Construction/Contratistas** (Highest Margin)
- **Segment size:** ~300 firms in Chile (10-200 people)
- **Pain point:** "Licitaciones are 30-50% of our revenue; we miss them or lose money"
- **Current process:** 1-2 people spend 2-3 days evaluating each base → errors
- **Agent impact:** Faster evaluation + fewer missed opportunities + lower bid-lose ratio

**Messaging:**
> "Stop losing licitaciones to missed requirements. Evaluate bases in 24 hours instead of 3 days."

**Entry conversation:**
- "How many licitaciones do you evaluate per month?"
- "How many do you pass on (no-bid)?"
- "How many do you bid but lose?"
- "What's the cost of missing a licitación vs. bidding wrong?"

**Pilot offer:**
- Free evaluation of 5 random bases (shows accuracy + insights)
- Then $299/month for unlimited licitación analysis
- Success metric: "Reduce evaluation time by 50% + improve go/no-go accuracy"

**Expansion path:**
- Add: Supplier network (subcontractor availability + pricing)
- Add: Budget estimation (construction cost benchmarking)
- Add: Proposal generation (auto-fill response templates)

---

#### 2. **Proveedores del Estado** (Stable Revenue)
- **Segment size:** ~250 firms
- **Pain point:** "State procurement is complex; documentation errors cost us contracts"
- **Current process:** Compliance officer manually checks each requirement
- **Agent impact:** Zero missed requirements + systematic checklist

**Messaging:**
> "Government contracts require perfect execution. Never miss a requirement again."

**Pilot offer:**
- 3-month trial: $199/month (all licitaciones from portal)
- Integration with BCP (Boletín de Contrataciones Públicas)
- Success metric: "100% compliance on next 10 bids"

---

#### 3. **Servicios / Logística** (Volume Play)
- **Segment size:** ~400 firms
- **Pain point:** "We bid a lot but don't know why we lose; some bids cost us money"
- **Current process:** Gut feeling on go/no-go; sparse evaluation
- **Agent impact:** Data-driven bidding; avoid money-losing bids

**Messaging:**
> "Bid smarter. Know before you bid whether you'll win + make money."

---

### **Sales Process**

1. **Discovery** (15 min call)
   - "Walk me through your last licitación process"
   - "Which step takes the most time?"
   - "How many bids do you lose? Why?"

2. **Demo** (Schedule live analysis)
   - Share 1-2 recent bases
   - Show agent output (go/no-go, checklist, risks)
   - Ask: "Is this useful? What would change if you had this 48 hours after bases drop?"

3. **Pilot** (30 days)
   - All licitaciones evaluated
   - Weekly reflection: "What did we learn?"
   - Go/no-go accuracy vs. your gut

4. **Expansion**
   - Integrate with vendor system (auto-pull from BCP)
   - Add proposal templates + budget estimation

## Playbook #3: Twin de Cobranza Pyme

### **Target Segments (Priority Order)**

#### 1. **Retail B2B / Distribución** (Highest Impact)
- **Segment size:** ~800 firms
- **Pain point:** "DSO is 60-75 days; we're financing our customers; cash is tight"
- **Current process:** Accountant sends late notices; no real follow-up discipline
- **Agent impact:** DSO → 45-50 days = $200k+ freed up cash (typical)

**Messaging:**
> "Recover $100k+ in aging receivables. Free up working capital without new financing."

**Entry conversation:**
- "What's your current days sales outstanding?"
- "How much do you estimate is aging >60 days?"
- "Who manages collection—accounting or operations?"
- "If we could reduce DSO by 15 days, what's that worth?"

**Pilot offer:**
- Free analysis of current aged receivables (shows potential)
- Then $199/month for ongoing management
- Integration: Excel upload (weekly) or API to accounting software
- Success metric: "Reduce DSO by 10 days in 60 days"

**Expansion path:**
- Integrated with bank (auto-flag slow payers)
- Judgment collection playbook (post-legal action)
- Customer segmentation (VIP treatment vs. firm collection)

---

#### 2. **Servicios Recurrentes** (Churn Prevention)
- **Segment size:** ~600 firms (cleaning, maintenance, staffing, consulting)
- **Pain point:** "Churn is 5-8% monthly; we don't see it coming; invoices pile up"
- **Current process:** Invoice + hope; no tracking
- **Agent impact:** Predict churn + systematic follow-up

**Messaging:**
> "Stop customers from disappearing. Track promises + prevent churn before it happens."

**Pilot offer:**
- 30-day trial: $99/month (manage up to 100 active accounts)
- Daily churn risk alerts + follow-up templates
- Success metric: "Reduce churn by 20% in 60 days"

---

#### 3. **SaaS / Subscription** (Retention Lever)
- **Segment size:** ~150 Chilean SaaS companies
- **Pain point:** "We have good retention but loose money in DSO; can't optimize pricing without payment discipline"
- **Current process:** Dunning emails; manual follow-up on failed cards
- **Agent impact:** Reduce involuntary churn + payment friction

**Messaging:**
> "Maximize LTV. Reduce involuntary churn + payment friction."

**Pilot offer:**
- Free integration audit (how much are you losing to payment failures?)
- $299/month: automated dunning + payment recovery
- Success metric: "+3% net revenue retention"

---

### **Sales Process**

1. **Discovery**
   - "What's your current DSO / days to collect?"
   - "What % of invoices are >60 days old?"
   - "How much time do you spend on collection per week?"
   - "If you could free up $X in cash by next quarter, what would you do with it?"

2. **Quantification**
   - Show potential: "Typical firm like you has $150k in aging receivables"
   - ROI calc: "At 8% cost of financing, that's $1,200/month you're paying in interest"

3. **Pilot**
   - Start with worst 20 accounts
   - Use agent for 30 days
   - Track recovery + DSO improvement

4. **Expansion**
   - Roll to all accounts
   - Add integrations (bank, accounting software)
   - Add judgment collection (lawyer partnerships)
